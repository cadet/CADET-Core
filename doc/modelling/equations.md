  component_li: $c^\ell_i$ concentration of component $i$ in the liquid phase.
  component_sij: $c^s_{j,i,m_{j,i}}$ concentration of component $i$ in the solid phase
    of particle type $j$ and particle type $m_{j,i}$.
  flow_in_out: $F_{\text{in}}$ and $F_{\text{out}}$ flow rates of liquid into and
    out of the tank.
  reaction: $f_{\text{react},i}^l\left( c^\ell \right)$ and $f_{\text{react},j,i}^s\left(
    c^\ell, c_j^s \right)$ reaction rates of component $i$ in the liquid and solid
    phase.
  reaction_ads: $f_{\text{ads},j}\left( c^\ell, c^s_j\right)$ adsorption rate of component
    $i$ in the solid phase of particle type $j$ .
  volume_fac_pat: $d_j$ volume fraction of the different solid volume in the tank.
  volume_liquid: $V^{\ell}$ liquid volume in the tank.
  volume_solid: $V^{s}$ solid volume in the tank.
